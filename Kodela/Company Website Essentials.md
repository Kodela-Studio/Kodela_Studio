### Website Pages I'd Include

#### 1. Home

Your first impression.

Include:

- Clear headline ("We design websites people actually enjoy using.")
- What you do
- Featured projects
- Testimonials
- Call-to-action ("Book a discovery call")

#### 2. About Us

One of the most visited pages.

Include:

- Your story
- Photos of all three founders
- Skills and specialties
- Why you started the company

People buy from people.

#### 3. Services

Examples:

- UX Design
- UI Design
- Website Design
- Front-End Development
- Website Audits
- Branding & Visual Identity
- Figma Design Systems
- Accessibility Reviews

#### 4. Portfolio / Projects

Probably your most important page.

For each project:

- Client goal
- Your process
- Before/after
- Screenshots
- Results

#### 5. Process

Clients love knowing what happens after they hire you.

Example:

1. Discovery
2. Research
3. Wireframes
4. Design
5. Development
6. Launch
7. Support

#### 6. Testimonials

Social proof sells.

#### 7. Contact

Include:

- Contact form
- Email
- Calendly/booking link
- Social links

#### 8. Blog (Optional)

Great for SEO.

Topics:

- UX tips
- Website mistakes
- Accessibility
- Design trends
- Small business website advice

## Things That Make Small Agencies Look Premium

- Professional photos of the founders
- Consistent color palette
- Strong portfolio
- Clear pricing or starting prices
- Case studies instead of just screenshots
- Easy booking process
- A memorable company name
